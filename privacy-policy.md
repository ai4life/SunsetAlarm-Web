# Chrome Web Store Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 14, 2025

## Privacy Policy for Sunset Alarm Chrome Extension

### Information We Collect

**Location Data:**
- Sunset Alarm requests access to your device's geolocation to calculate accurate sunset times for your specific location
- This location data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to external servers
- Location data is not stored permanently and is only used for real-time sunset calculations

**No Personal Information:**
- We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information
- No user accounts, emails, names, or other personal data are required or collected
- No usage analytics or tracking data is collected

### How We Use Information

**Location Data Usage:**
- Your location coordinates are used solely to calculate accurate sunset times using astronomical algorithms
- Calculations are performed locally on your device using the SunCalc library
- Location data is used only when you actively use the extension and is not stored between sessions

### Data Storage and Security

**Local Storage Only:**
- All alarm settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
- No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties
- You have full control over your data and can clear it at any time by removing the extension

### Third-Party Services

**No External Dependencies:**
- Sunset Alarm operates entirely offline after installation
- No external APIs, analytics services, or tracking tools are used
- No data is shared with third parties under any circumstances

### Your Rights and Controls

**Full Control:**
- You can revoke location permission at any time through Chrome's extension settings
- You can uninstall the extension at any time to remove all associated data
- All data processing happens transparently on your device

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

### Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the extension's data practices, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support channels.

---

**Summary:** Sunset Alarm is designed with privacy in mind. We only access your location to calculate sunset times locally on your device, never store personal information, and never transmit data to external servers. Your privacy is completely protected.
